Judo For Schools

Teachers

The benefits of Judo in schools is becoming better known and understood by head teachers, teachers and parents. Join the growing number of schools who have added Judo as part of the curriculum.

The improvements that Judo can bring go far beyond fitness and the ethos of Judo can go hand in hand with anti-bullying strategies and used as evidence during Ofsted inspections. Other benefits of teaching Judo in schools are:

  • Improved fitness
  • Better flexibility
  • Respect for other
  • Respect for boundaries
  • Self esteem
  • Self confidence
  • Higher school attendance
  • Better school grades

Sports clubs across the country have seen a surge in interest fuelled by the 2012 Olympics.
