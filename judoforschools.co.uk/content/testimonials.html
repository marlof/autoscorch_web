Testimonials

Our children thrive under Adrian’s direction where he has been Sensei of our after school Judo Club for nearly four years. He is passionate about his sport and infects his students with the same enthusiasm. The children are always pleased to see him on Friday afternoon and eager to get changed and ready to start. The club is vibrant and the children thoroughly enjoy the hour they spend under his direction.
 
He has a gentle but commanding manner that engenders their respect and in turn he models the respect he expects the children to show each other. This completely supports and upholds our school ethos.
Adrian has an innate ability of seeing the potential in every child irrespective of their ability and helps them to develop their skills through encouragement, instruction and coaching.
 
He has delivered assembly to three hundred children where he has truly engaged and enthused them while celebrating the achievements of the children in the club through presenting awards. 
 
I am delighted at the way the judo club under Adrian’s coaching benefits our children,  he is such an asset to our school community.
 
Christine Lynch, Head Teacher
Heatherside Infant School

 

Judo for Schools have been providing the children of Heatherside Junior School with the opportunity to learn and develop their skills for several years. Recently, Sensei Adrian provided the whole school with a taster session as part of the schools aim to introduce children to a wider variety of sport. Since then, the after school club has seen a noticeable increase in participation and now only has one space available!

Sensei Adrian places a great deal of importance on fitness and motivation amongst young children. This also transfers over to adults, as the staff found out during a P.E. focused Inset day!
 
Miss Campbell, P.E. Co-ordinator
30th January 2014
 

The Dove brothers are, without doubt, two of the best coaches I have encountered in my many years of martial arts experience.   Judo for Schools is run in the most professional and respectful manner and through their passion for Judo the coaches instill confidence, discipline, and respect into all of their students, as well as improve their general health and fitness to an exceptional level. 

Judo carries many fitness benefits for the individual, including functional strength, flexibility, cardiovascular fitness, muscular endurance, power, agility, toughness, core strength and stability to name but a few.  The life skills I've seen taught through the coaches guidance are equally impressive such as self confidence, respect, discipline, aggression and emotional control, team spirit, social cohesion and much more. 
 
It is an honour to be working with this exceptional Team and Club. I would whole heartedly recommend Judo For Schools and Judo Club Osaka to any parent looking for a suitable venue for their child to attend.
 
"I consider myself very fortunate to have OSAKA Judo club operating out of Fight Science Gym in Aldershot."
 
Nick Chapman
Managing Director
Fight Science Ltd.
